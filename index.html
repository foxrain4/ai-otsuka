Ai Otsuka (大塚 愛)

Japanese singer-songwriter, composer, and actress known for her heartfelt lyrics and bubbly energy.

Biography

Ai Otsuka debuted in 2003 with "Momo no Hanabira" and rose to fame with her hit single "Sakuranbo." Known for blending pop with unique storytelling, she has established herself as one of Japan’s beloved pop artists.
